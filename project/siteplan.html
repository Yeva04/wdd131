Madagascar Wildlife Hub - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Madagascar Wildlife Hub

Description: The name reflects a digital hub dedicated to Madagascar’s unique wildlife and conservation efforts. It emphasizes biodiversity and community engagement, fostering recognition among conservationists and enthusiasts. The logo will feature a lemur silhouette, symbolizing Madagascar’s iconic species.

Site Purpose

The Madagascar Wildlife Hub provides an informational platform to raise awareness about Madagascar’s biodiversity, focusing on endangered species like lemurs and chameleons. It offers resources on conservation efforts, volunteer opportunities, and donation options, alongside interactive species profiles to engage visitors and promote action.

Scenarios

  • How can I volunteer with conservation projects in Madagascar?
  • Which species are most at risk, and how can I support their protection?

Color Schema

Forest Green (#2E7D32): Used for headers, backgrounds, and buttons to evoke Madagascar’s lush rainforests.

Earth Brown (#5D4037): Applied to body text and accents, symbolizing the island’s rich soil and grounding the design.

Typography

Roboto (Body): A clean, modern sans-serif font for body text, ensuring readability across devices.

Merriweather (Headings): A serif font for headings, adding elegance and emphasis to titles and subheadings.

Wireframes

Mobile View

- Header: Logo (lemur silhouette) at top-left, hamburger menu at top-right (Forest Green background).
- Navigation: Collapsed menu (expands on click) with links: Home, Species, Conservation, Get Involved.
- Main: Hero image (rainforest), welcome text, featured species card (image, name, status).
- Footer: Contact info, social media icons, copyright (Earth Brown text, Forest Green background).

Desktop View

- Header: Centered logo, horizontal navigation bar below (Home, Species, Conservation, Get Involved).
- Navigation: Inline links, hover effect (Earth Brown underline).
- Main: Full-width hero image, two-column layout: left (welcome text), right (three species cards in grid).
- Footer: Three columns: Contact, Social Media, Copyright (same colors as mobile).
